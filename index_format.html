My work

I work as a Data Science Intern for the Callysto Project, a federally-funded program that supports Grades 5-12 students and teachers across Canada. I also hold a part time position supporting DevOps-related tasks for the iReceptor Project, a platform whose goal is facilitating access to a distributed network of AIRR-seq repositories.


Vision

I love working on problems that involve data: finding, parsing, cleaning, visualizing, analysing and interpreting. I also have a keen interest in using mathematics to model various phenomena. My hope is for my work to inspire people who form part of minority groups to take leadership and professional roles in the sciences.
